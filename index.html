About

I'm Chenika, a passionate software engineer with a burning desire to make a meaningful impact through technology. As a recipient of the full-ride Pearson scholarship, I had the incredible opportunity to pursue my studies at the renowned University of Toronto. This invaluable experience laid the foundation for my love for computer science. During my time at UofT, I've immersed myself in various areas of this ever-evolving field. Ask me about my expereince in the UoftAI club, where I explored the captivating realm of artificial intelligence! In my quest for continuous growth, I ventured into the exciting world of cybersecurity, earning my Certified Ethical Hacking certification. This enabled me to develop a keen understanding of safeguarding digital systems and bolstering their resilience against potential threats.

I was selected as to join the Technology Leadership Initiative (TLI) at UofT. This exclusive program brought together 30 exceptional computer scientists from the entire Computer Science class at UofT, fostering collaboration and innovation. Together, we collaborated with the company VoiceFlow on problems they were facing during chatbot developement. During this journey I honed my web-dev skills and became proficient in React and Node.

In the summer of 2023, I joined TD Bank as a software engineer on a full-stack agile team. During this time I've continued my growth as a web-developer and agile team-member. Ask me more about this experience!

Driven by an insatiable curiosity, I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my skill set. I approach each project with a growth mindset, embracing collaboration and constantly pushing myself to deliver top-notch solutions. With a strong foundation in computer science, a thirst for knowledge, and a passion for problem-solving, I am eager to contribute my skills and drive to a dynamic team. I am excited about the prospect of making a real difference through technology and creating innovative solutions that positively impact lives.
